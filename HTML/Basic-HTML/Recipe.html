Refreshing Lemon Juice

This homemade lemon juice is tangy, sweet, and perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh lemons
  • 3 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the lemons in half and squeeze out the juice.
  2. Strain the juice to remove seeds and pulp.
  3. Mix the lemon juice with cold water in a jug.
  4. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  5. Serve in glasses with ice cubes and garnish with mint leaves.
